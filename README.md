# E-Com Xpress Sales Analysis Dashboard (2024–2025)

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze sales performance for E-Com Xpress during 2024–2025. The dashboard provides insights into sales trends, product performance, regional sales distribution, and revenue loss analysis, helping businesses make data-driven decisions.

## Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance.
* Identify top-performing products.
* Monitor monthly sales trends.
* Evaluate state-wise sales contribution.
* Analyze revenue loss by phone brands.
* Track progress against sales targets.

## Dashboard Highlights

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Sales: 2 Billion+
* Sales Target Achievement Analysis
* Product Performance Tracking
* Regional Sales Distribution

### Visualizations Included

1. **Total Sales by Year, Quarter, and Month**

   * Monthly sales trend analysis.
   * Identifies seasonal fluctuations and growth patterns.

2. **Total Sales by Product Name**

   * Comparison of product-wise sales performance.
   * Highlights top-selling products.

3. **Lost Revenue by Phone Brand**

   * Revenue loss distribution across different phone brands.
   * Helps identify areas for improvement.

4. **Total Sales by State**

   * State-wise contribution to total sales.
   * Supports regional business strategy planning.
